A master class in world building

Reviewed at: Ch52 Mystery Meat Machine
by The one beyond the veil Wednesday, March 29, 2023 10:11:05 PM

The story is phenomenally well written, and even after coming off a nearly two-year hiatus, the flow and tone of the world around MC is so consistently good I keep coming back again and again. The story is well served by the MC being as ignorant as the readers, as the world he finds himself in is dense and unfamiliar to many established tropes and cliches, and learning alongside him has been half the fun. 

To that end I think Super Minion has even established itself as a unique super sub-genre, building upon the blocks laid by predecessor stories and cementing a tradition of similar worlds in other stories that rightfully recognize all the things that make Fortress City great.

Told from a non-human's perspective very well

Reviewed at: Ch45 Bloodsuckers
by MeisterKleister Saturday, September 5, 2020 9:51:50 PM

Easily my favorite story on this website so far. It's about an artificial being hiding among humans in a world where super heroes/villains exist.

I love the non-human protagonist's anthropological observations and how he slowly learns to be human. He's rational, logical, and very believably written, in my opinion. He's strong, but not too strong.

The only downside I can think of is that his goals seem to be to survive, be safe, and grow stronger. And the plot so far is about facing the threats and problems as they emerge.
